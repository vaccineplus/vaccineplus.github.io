Key Features

Digitize Your Records

Remember where you put your vaccine card? Is it lost or damaged? VaccinePlus eliminates the hassles of paper records, encrypting your data securely and permanently on the blockchain. Only authorized parties can view certain data. With blockchain, your records are instantly synced among and trusted by network peers like hospitals and schools, saving time and money.

Book & Track Your Vaccine

Don’t run the risk of getting an improperly handled, adulterated or fake vaccine. When manufacturers supply vaccines to clinics and hospitals, it’s registered and tracked on our tamper-proof, public ledger. Book your vaccination with our network of clinics, and rest assured knowing that you’ll be getting an authentic, safe product.

Get Vaccine Advice

Receive personalized reminders and tips pre and post-vaccination. Get timely updates based on public health recommendations from real medical doctors and associations in North America, Europe and Asia. [maybe can combine it with Monitor Disease Outbreaks]

Monitor Disease Outbreaks

With measles outbreaks and novel viruses on the rise, it pays to know who’s protected, who’s at risk and where it’s safe to go. Learn about the latest research on vaccines, emerging diseases and public health issues in a format you and your family can understand.
